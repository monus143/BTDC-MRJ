About Us

Welcome to BTDC MRJ

Bhabha Technical Degree College has been established for this purpose. That the unemployed young men/women here acquire technical education, become self-reliant with this technical knowledge, and contribute to the development of their society and their country while living a respectable life.

We thought any unemployed young man/girl who gets technical education from here should not feel incomplete. Because many institutions run in our country to distribute degrees, but we want to provide that technical knowledge which will make the unemployed young men/women self-reliant. What is needed is self-knowledge, loyalty and fighting dedication.
